About Us

HELLO!

I'm Cyrille. A college student, currently residing in Manila. Crocheting started as my hobby, then it abruptly became a profitable side hustle. I create Amigurumi dolls and crochet flowers.

Amigurumi (Japanese: 編みぐるみ, lit. "crocheted or knitted stuffed toy") is the Japanese art of knitting or crocheting small, stuffed yarn creatures. I can make customized dolls based on your preferences, and all sorts of crochet flowers too!
